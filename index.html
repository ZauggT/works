About Me

I am a dedicated Data Designer from Switzerland. My passion lies in using programming to transform data into captivating visualizations. I've learned how powerful data can be when presented effectively and believe it can tell compelling stories and drive change. My main focus is finding innovative ways to present complex data clearly.

My goal as a Data Designer is to create meaningful impact, whether addressing social, environmental, or economic issues. I'm committed to continuous learning and eagerly anticipate contributing to the data-driven world of tomorrow through inspiring projects and visualizations.
